Hello!
I’m Cathleen.


UX/UI Designer


I use design thinking to create delightful experiences in digital and physical spaces for fellow humans.

My curiosity has taken me across industries and continents.


I have backgrounds in marketing, retail merchandising, hospitality, and visual design. I was a visual merchandiser, public relations manager, marketing manager, customer experience manager, and graphic designer. These experiences have given me perspective and skills that are useful to me as a UX/UI designer.

Why
UX/UI
design?

I love being around people, and understanding what motivates them energizes me.


I enjoy helping people harness the power of technology to unlock boundaries and amplify creativity. The process of building technology informed by a deep understanding of human needs excites me. The combination of the three is ultimately what led me to UX/UI Design.

If I’m not sorting stickies or pushing pixels around, you’ll find me practicing yoga or at the kickboxing gym.


Though it’s easy to fall in and out of routine, getting back into practice always grounds me. Earning my 200-hour Yoga Teacher Training (YTT) certification during the pandemic was one of the most challenging and rewarding experiences yet.
